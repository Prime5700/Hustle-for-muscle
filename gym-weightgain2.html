Science Of Weight Gain

The best way to build muscle is with resistance training and progressive overload. Different programs target different goals for hypertrophy (mass) versus strength. People new to resistance training should focus on building a decent base level of strength and muscle definition, as well as practicing good form, before trying to add specificity to their program.

Muscle is made when you eat and sleep: Nutrition and recovery are extremely important for people who lift weights.

Human muscles are combination of large and small fibres of muscles,when we target specific muscle through weight resistance training than the fibers of that specific muscle is torn apart and needs recovery witin 72 hours thus to fullfill such need you are advised to eat a perfect balanced diet with increased quantity of proteins.

How To Do

In order to gain weight

  • Warm up your body with push-ups,pull-ups,sit-ups and stretching
  • Train only single part of body in a day if you are beginner
  • You may train two parts in a day as you become intermediate
  • Perform 6 to 7 Exercises of large muscles [Chest,Back and Legs]
  • Perform 4 to 5 Exercises of small muscles [Biceps,Triceps and Shoulders]
  • There are 3 sets to perform a exercise
  • The weight should be minimum in first set
  • The weight should be maximum in Last set

First week

A beginner cannot gain muscles in first week as body is trying to adapt the new routine of weight training.It is highly recommended that you perform only one set of exercise of every body part [reffered as mixed days]

Focus more on basic and low weight practise sets and perform stretching as many as possible
